AKAN NAMES

Akan Names

In Ghanaian culture, newborns are usually given names
that correspond to the day of the week in which they were born.

Male Akan Names

  1. Monday-Kwadwo
  2. Tuesday-Kwabena
  3. Wednesday- Kwaku
  4. Thursday- Yaw
  5. Friday-Kofi
  6. Saturday-Kwame
  7. Sunday-Kwasi

Female Akan Names

  1. Monday-Adwoa
  2. Tuesday-Abenaa
  3. Wednesday-Akua
  4. Thursday- Yaa
  5. Friday-Afua
  6. Saturday-Ama
  7. Sunday-Akosua
